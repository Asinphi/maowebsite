About Us

Cypress Bay Science National Honor Society (SNHS) seeks to champion the importance of the sciences and provide learning opportunities for all students. We organize activities that further the student body's knowledge of science as well as promote passion and interest for its many fields.

Requirements to Join


  • A cumulative unweighted GPA of at least 3.0.
  • An unweighted GPA of at least 3.5 in all science courses.
  • Completion of at least 2 Honors, Advanced Placement, or AICE science courses.
